Welcome To Deceitful Dice!

Hello! Do you like lying to people?

Then you have come to the right place!

Deceitful Dice is the game for you!

Take turns trying to trick you opponent into calling you a liar!

Play against a computer opponent and see how your skills match up to them!

Instructions for playing the game
  • Click on Menu and Start Game
  • Once a game starts, ten dice will be rolled.
  • Each player will start with five dice each.
  • The opponent's dice will be hidden from the other.
  • At the start of a turn, a player will place a bet.
  • A bet will consist of the number of dice and a single value of those dice.
  • The value of the dice will be from one to six.
  • The number of dice is the claim that those dice exist within the entire dice pool.
  • After making a bet, the opponent will have two choices:
    1. Make a new higher bet.
    2. Call your opponent a liar.
  • To make a higher bet, the number of dice must be higher if the value stays the same.
  • Or the value must be at least one dice higher.
  • If a player is called a liar, the round is over and the bet is checked.
  • All the dice are shown to both players.
  • The number of dice in the bet is checked against the dice pool.
  • If the bet is at least equal to the dice in the pool, then that player wins that round. Otherwise, the betting player loses the round.
  • The loser of the round will lose a single piece of dice.
  • A new round starts and this continues until a player loses all of their dice.
  • You can save your game at anytime when quitting.
